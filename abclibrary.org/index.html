Our mission is to supply positive diverse representation and antiracism childrens books to classrooms and early education centers.

We exist to build an antiracist future.

With your donations, ABC will supply books to classrooms with positive diverse representation childrens books. We believe that we can imbue the values of equity and inclusion at a young age when

How it works:

Step 1

Visit the ABC Library on our website. Open access to all to use as a resource - browse through!

Step 2

Find books for your classroom from our searchable database or our pre-curated lists.

Step 3

Fill out an order form. (Note: due to high demand, we anticipate a waitlist for our initial launch)

Step 4

Receive your books in the mail and enjoy positive diverse representation books in your classrooms!

At ABC, we are always learning and growing

We add to the ABC Library on a regular basis. If we dont have a book that you would like to see included, please let us know! Send your suggestions to [email protected].

We are committed to continue learning and to always strive to do better. We are not perfect, nor do we claim that the ABC Library is perfect. We launched this project as a channel to donate books to budget-constrained classrooms through an efficient database, but we have NOT read through every book. If you find a book that is problematic or if you see an issue with our categorization or description that needs to be corrected, please email [email protected]. We try our best to be knowledgeable and empathetic, and we hold ourselves accountable to addressing and fixing any issues.

Together, we move closer to an antiracist future. Thank you for your support!
